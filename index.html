Rice — 10 kg ❌ Leave behind

FairPrice, Sheng Siong, and Mustafa Centre all stock Cambodian jasmine rice. S$15–25 for 10 kg, delivered same day on Lazada.

Rice cooker ❌ Buy in SG

Singapore is Type G (UK 3-pin, 230V). A Tiger or Zojirushi on Lazada SG costs S$50–120. Not worth 3.5 kg of freight on a used appliance.

Power adapters (7-hole) ❌ Buy in SG

Singapore IS the 3-pin UK standard. Buy a proper SG extension lead on Lazada for S$10–15 — safer, lighter, correct spec.

Bicycle via air freight ❌ Separate action

Non-folding + child seat = oversized, cannot go in 100 kg company freight. See Freight tab for your three options.

Heavy gym equipment ❌ Buy in SG

Dumbbells, kettlebells = dead weight in freight. Decathlon SG pricing is fair. Many HDB estates have a free community gym.

Large furniture ❌ Buy / source in SG

IKEA SG, Lazada, and Carousell SG cover furniture well. Budget S$2,000–3,000 to furnish the HDB. 95A Henderson at $4,500 fully furnished avoids this cost.

Mustafa Centre in Little India (open 24/7, 145 Syed Alwi Road) is your first stop in Week 1 — South and Southeast Asian food brands, electronics, household items at prices well below supermarkets.
